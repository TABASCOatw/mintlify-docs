Universal Accounts support a wide range of blockchain networks, enabling users to interact across different networks with a single account and balance.

## Supported Networks

- **Solana**
- **Ethereum**
- **Base**
- **BNB Chain**
- **Sonic**
- **Berachain**
- **Avalanche**
- **Arbitrum One**
- **Polygon**
- **Linea**
- **Blast**
- **Conflux eSpace**
- **Manta Pacific**
- **OP**
- **Mode**
- **Merlin**

<Note>
This list is regularly updated as new chains are integrated into the Universal Accounts infrastructure. All supported chains can be accessed through a single Universal Account without requiring separate wallet setups.
</Note>
